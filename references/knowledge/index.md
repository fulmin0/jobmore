## Priority Reading Order (by job search stage)

**Before applying (any role)**:
1. Ch17 (Appendix) — internalize McAllister, Norton, Nash, LPs
2. Ch11 (Define Yourself) — narrative and positioning
3. Ch7 (Resumes) — 5 rules

**Before an interview**:
4. Ch12 (Behavioral) — STAR + story bank prep
5. Ch14 (Product Questions) — user-first framework
6. Ch10 (Company Research) — research dimensions

**For specific question types**:
7. Ch13 (Estimation) — if JD is analytics-heavy
8. Ch15 (Case Questions) — if Amazon or strategy PM
9. Ch9 (Cover Letters) — before writing any cover letter
10. Ch2 (PM Role) — for identity/career questions

**Lower priority**:
- Ch3, Ch4, Ch5, Ch6, Ch8, Ch16 — contextually useful; not required prep
